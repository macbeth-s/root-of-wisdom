# Quilaco's Halberd
The golden weapon of the Fourfold Princes and their most esteemed commanders.
Initial Location: Armory
## Consider
When Leonor first fled the Rock of the Owl, she was entrusted this by Quilaco, with the promise to someday return it to his hands. Even after centuries of blood and rot, it still shines bright.
**Effect:** [Memory: Sight](https://uadaf.theevilroot.xyz/rowenarium/elements/mem.sight)
