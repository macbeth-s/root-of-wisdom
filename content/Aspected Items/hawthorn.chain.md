# Hawthorn Chain
The wood splinters and scrapes any who would dare uncoil this bind.
## Consider
The Horned-Axe maintains division and prevents encroachment. Let what is closed off remain closed.
<br>**Effect**: [Memory: Touch](https://uadaf.theevilroot.xyz/rowenarium/element/mem.touch)

- Knock 4
- Winter 2
- Scale 2
- Heart 2
- #Coil 
- #Wooden 
- #Thing

**Start:** Whittle the wood into a coarse Knock-binding.	<br>**Finish:** \[With Keeper-level Heart, the Chain is the precursor to Quilaco's Lazo.]
